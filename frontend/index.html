FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AtlasPi?

AtlasPi is a premium discovery platform created to help pioneers find trusted places, services and merchant opportunities related to Pi-oriented commerce.

Who is AtlasPi for?

AtlasPi is designed for pioneers, merchants, creators, community leaders and anyone interested in real-world Pi discovery.

Is AtlasPi an official Pi Core Team product?

No. AtlasPi is an independent community-focused project built to support visibility, trust and discovery around Pi commerce.

Why is AtlasPi important?

Because discoverability and trust are key to helping pioneers explore practical utility in a clearer way.
